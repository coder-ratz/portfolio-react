At the beginning of 2021, as a leading member of a working group, I developed a local intranet site using Microsoft SharePoint. We began by writing user stories, developing a basic structure, and designing while exploring the capabilities of SharePoint and Office 365.

Developing based on user stories

Whiteboard view, Faiveley Transport Bochum GmbH

The project progressed swiftly, and already by mid-2021, we launched the intranet site. To ensure user adoption, I conducted a live demonstration for over 200 colleagues showcasing its functionalities. The intranet site received growing interest from various contributors and was rated nearly 5/5 in a user poll.

I enjoyed the collaboration during the development phase and the direct user feedback. As of early 2024, I continue to maintain the site as an admin, regularly adding new content and features.
